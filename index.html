The problem

That CVE from last Tuesday? It's still unpatched on 12 client endpoints. You don't know which ones.

You manage patching for 30 clients across 1,200 endpoints. Your RMM says 94% patch compliance. But 94% means 72 machines are running with known vulnerabilities right now. You don't know which clients, which machines, or which CVEs. When the breach happens, the client won't ask about your 94%. They'll ask why that one machine wasn't patched.

01

Missed patch tests = liability. You schedule a patch test but lose track of the 48-hour window. Client books before results are confirmed. Now you're scrambling to reschedule and document everything.

02

Paper consent forms get lost. You email a form, client prints it, signs it, but never sends it back. You have no proof of consent, and your liability insurance doesn't cover it.

03

Touch-up reminders fall through the cracks. You manually track when clients need touch-ups, but by week 6 you've forgotten. Clients don't book their follow-ups, and you leave money on the table.

04

No single view of every client's status. You switch between Instagram DMs, text, booking tool, and a spreadsheet. You never know who's completed what, and compliance audits are a nightmare.

How it works

Three simple steps to compliance done.

Step 1

See exactly which endpoints are exposed and which CVEs are unpatched, now

When a client books in your calendar, PatchBook automatically schedules their patch test 48 hours before the appointment. You get a notification, and the client gets a confirmation text with the date and instructions.

Step 2

Know which clients are at risk before a breach forces the conversation

PatchBook sends a digital consent form to your client's phone. They sign on their device, you get a timestamped record, and it's stored securely. No lost paperwork, full compliance audit trail.

Step 3

Prove your patch compliance to clients with data they can verify

4-6 weeks after the appointment, PatchBook automatically sends your client a reminder to book their touch-up. They reply with their availability, and you recover those touch-up bookings without lifting a finger.
